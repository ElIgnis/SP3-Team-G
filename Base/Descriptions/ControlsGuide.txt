------------Controls------------
 Movement:
   'W' - Moves the character UP
   'S' - Moves the character Left
   'A' - Moves the character Down
   'D' - Moves the character Right
 Interactions:
   'Spacebar' - Stun enemies around player
   '1-9' - Use the highlighted item in the
	  hotbar.
   'V' - Disguise
   'B' - Drop Decoy
